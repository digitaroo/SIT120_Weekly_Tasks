The minimum rate of pay an employee must be paid by an employer is determined by:

  1. The registered agreement covering the employer and employee (see How awards apply), or otherwise
  2. The modern award providing coverage by (see How awards apply), or otherwise...
  3. The national minimum wage (see Award & agreement free wages & conditions)
